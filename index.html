About Us

HACC, a student-led organization, serves as a hub for aspiring programmers and tech enthusiasts. Their primary mission is to foster a vibrant community where individuals can collaborate, learn, and grow their skills in the realm of computer science and coding. Through the organization of hackathons, competitions, and coding contests, HACC provides a dynamic platform for students to showcase their talents and challenge themselves to solve complex problems...

Our Work

At HACC, we engage in various coding projects, participate in hackathons, and contribute to open-source communities. Our focus is on learning, building, and collaborating on technology-driven projects.
